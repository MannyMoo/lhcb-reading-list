There are also theory papers. In particular, we follow some papers by Robert Fleischer that detail some of the consequences of two body decays of B mesons, but these can be quite heavy going:

  • Rare Decays and CP Violation in the Bs System (this is a nice review)

  • Branching Ratio Measurements of Bs Decays (branching ratio measurements of heavy and light states)

  • Effective Lifetimes of Bs Decays and their Constraints on the Bs0 − B̄s0 Mixing Parameters (effective lifetimes)

  • In Pursuit of New Physics With Bs0 → K+K− (new physics in Bs->KK decays)

  • Bs, d → ππ, πK, KK: Status and Prospects (B and Bs_>hh)
